Welcome. I'm Guadalupe Viscarra — Lupe to friends and readers — and this is where I share the ideas that changed how I think about life, work, and building something meaningful online.

If you've landed here, you're probably searching for more than another productivity hack. You want permission to follow your heart, tools to stay motivated when things get hard, and a reminder that you have more power to reshape your life than you think. That's exactly what this collection of insights is about.

## Follow Your Heart

Two voices shaped this philosophy for me more than any others: Steve Jobs and Alan Watts.

Jobs closed his famous Stanford commencement address with four words that still stop me in my tracks: **"Stay hungry. Stay foolish."** It's a call to keep learning, keep risking, and never settle into comfort just because it's easy.

Alan Watts asked a question that cuts even deeper: *"Better a short life that is full of what you like doing than a long life spent in a miserable way."*

Don't let fear, other people's expectations, or the safe path talk you out of what actually matters to you. The goal isn't to endure life — it's to fill it with work, relationships, and pursuits that feel genuinely yours.

## Rethink What You Believe

You can hold your own personal beliefs and still question everything around you. Real growth starts when we stop accepting "that's just how things are" and start asking *why*.

We're in the middle of a shift in consciousness. More people are waking up to the idea that the default script — school, job, retire, repeat — isn't the only option. That shift in energy is real, and it opens space for you to design a different reality.

You don't have to live inside someone else's imagination of what's possible for you. Carve your own niche. Build something that outlasts a single season. Dream the dream — even when the people around you can't see it yet.

## Embrace Every Emotion

We spend so much of our lives concealing how we really feel — smoothing things over, staying positive, pushing through. Then we wonder why stress, anxiety, and depression pile up.

What if the answer isn't to resist difficult emotions but to stop fighting them? What if feeling fully — including the uncomfortable parts — is what frees you?

Love is our highest emotion. The key isn't to stop feeling. It's to start. When you allow yourself to experience what's true, you stop leaking energy into suppression and start channeling it into change.

## Activate Your Millionaire Mind

Mindset comes before money. Always.

T. Harv Eker built his reputation on a bold claim: give him five minutes and he could predict your financial future — not through magic, but by understanding the beliefs running underneath your decisions.

The lesson isn't about chasing wealth for its own sake. It's about identifying the stories you inherited about money, success, and what you're "allowed" to have. Change the mindset, and the behavior follows. Invest in yourself — it's often the highest-return investment you'll ever make.

## Realize the Power You Have to Change

Life is short. You never know how much time you have left.

Humans have a remarkable ability most of us underuse: the power to change direction in an instant through a single decision. Not someday. Not after one more year of preparation. Now.

Become the best version of yourself — not eventually, but starting today. The new year gets all the attention, but any ordinary Tuesday is a valid day to choose differently.

## Stay Motivated Every Day

Some days you need a speech. Other days, a single sentence is enough. Here are a few that have carried me through:

> "Each one of us has a fire in our heart for something. It's our goal in life to find it and keep it lit." — Mary Lou Retton

> "To exist is to change, to change is to mature, to mature is to go on creating oneself endlessly." — Henri Bergson

Visual quotes — especially on platforms like Instagram — hit differently. A picture really can tell a thousand words, and in a feed that gives you a second of attention, that's powerful. Share what moves you. Motivate others. The ripple effect is real.

## Make Money Online and Embrace Your Passion

So you want to make money online? Welcome to the club.

Almost everyone I meet wants the freedom lifestyle — location independence, work that aligns with who they are, income that doesn't require trading every hour for a paycheck. The internet made that possible in ways previous generations couldn't imagine.

But here's the part people skip: the most sustainable online businesses are built on passion, not gimmicks. Start with what you care about. Develop real expertise. Serve people who need what you know. The money follows genuine value more reliably than any shortcut.

Embrace your passion. Build around it. That's how you create work that doesn't feel like a long life spent in a miserable way.

## Dream Beyond Imagination

Are you surviving, or are you living? The rat race keeps you busy enough that you never stop to ask.

Create another reality for yourself. Don't accept the ceiling others place on your potential. Legacy isn't reserved for famous names — it's built by ordinary people who decide their work and their message matter.

Dream beyond what seems reasonable. Then take one step toward it today.
